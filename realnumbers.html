Euclid's Lemma

 For any two positive integers "a" and "b", there exists two unique integers q and r such that a=bq+r. Where 0≤ r < b

Euclid's Algorithm

An algorithm is a step by step statement to solve any problem. Eclid's algorith is used to find HCF(Highest Common factor) or gcd(greatest common divisor)of two numbers. It is based on Euclid's Lemma.
